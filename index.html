hi, i'm pie! i do art, i'm a d&d nerd, and i really like dogs. welcome to the commissions page!
commissions
- payment preferably done through paypal, in SGD. will be requested through an invoice if you wish.
- a sketch will be provided, then once approved, i'll start work on the proper piece.
- time taken varies, depending on workload and general irl stuff.
- feel free to ask for status updates over the course of the commission! average time taken is a week, but more complicated pieces will take longer.
- once a piece is complete, i'll still fix up minor details / colour adjustments for free. if my style has somehow also changed a lot between the time of commission and present time, i don't mind redoing the piece for free once. if you're not sure, just ask!
- i will draw: characters from movies, comic books, etc; idols; original characters; animals; monsters.
- i will not draw: nsfw, mecha, anthromorphic animals, overly complicated outfits.
